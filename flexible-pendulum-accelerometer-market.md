<p><p>The Flexible Pendulum Accelerometer market primarily consists of two types: Piezoelectric and Piezoresistive. Piezoelectric accelerometers utilize materials that generate an electrical charge when subjected to mechanical stress, making them highly sensitive and suitable for dynamic measurements. In contrast, Piezoresistive accelerometers rely on changes in electrical resistance due to applied force, offering robustness and stability in varying conditions. Both types serve diverse applications in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors, facilitating precise motion detection and analysis.</p></p>
