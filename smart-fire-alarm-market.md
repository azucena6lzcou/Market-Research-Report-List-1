<p><strong>The Smart Fire Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart fire alarm market consists of three primary types: photoelectric, dual sensors, and ionization alarms. Photoelectric alarms use a light-sensitive sensor to detect smoldering fires, making them ideal for use in areas with significant smoke production. Dual sensor alarms combine both photoelectric and ionization technology, providing comprehensive fire detection for various fire types. Ionization alarms, on the other hand, are more responsive to fast-flaming fires. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse safety needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
