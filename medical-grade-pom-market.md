<p><p>The medical grade POM (polyoxymethylene) market is divided into two primary types: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM is known for its high strength, rigidity, and low friction, making it suitable for precision components in medical devices. In contrast, copolymer POM exhibits enhanced resistance to impact and better dimensional stability, catering to applications where flexibility and toughness are essential. Both types are utilized in developing reliable, durable products for the medical industry, ensuring high performance and safety standards.</p></p>
