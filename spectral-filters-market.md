<p><p>The spectral filters market consists of several key types that cater to specific light manipulation needs. Dichroic filters selectively transmit certain wavelengths while reflecting others, ideal for applications in lighting and optics. Neutral density (ND) filters uniformly reduce light intensity without altering color, commonly used in photography and videography. Band-pass filters allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass while blocking others, essential in spectroscopy and imaging. Other market categories include color filters and variable filters, addressing diverse optical applications.</p></p>
