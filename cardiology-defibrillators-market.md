<p><p>The cardiology defibrillators market comprises three main types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), External Defibrillators, and Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs). ICDs are surgically implanted devices that monitor and restore normal heart rhythms. External defibrillators are portable devices used in emergencies to deliver shocks to a patient experiencing sudden cardiac arrest. WCDs are wearable devices designed for patients at risk of arrhythmias, providing continuous monitoring and shock delivery if needed, enhancing patient safety and management.</p></p>
