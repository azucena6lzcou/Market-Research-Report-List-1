<p><ul><li>MAO Inhibitors (Monoamine Oxidase)</li><li>AChE Inhibitors (Acetylcholinesterase)</li><li>Glutamate Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dementia and movement disorder treatments encompass several medication types targeting specific neurological pathways. MAO inhibitors enhance neurotransmitter levels by inhibiting monoamine oxidase, improving mood and cognitive function. AChE inhibitors increase acetylcholine availability, aiding memory and cognitive tasks by preventing its breakdown. Glutamate inhibitors regulate glutamate activity, which can help in controlling symptoms associated with neurodegenerative diseases. Together, these treatments aim to alleviate symptoms, improve patient quality of life, and slow disease progression in affected individuals.</p></p>
